About, in brief

I work at the intersection of product, data, and storytelling. Eight years of building data strategy and analytics products — most recently at NYU, where I lead enterprise data integrity, predictive modeling, and CRM modernization across a complex stakeholder landscape.

My MBA from NYU Stern focused on Technology Product Management, and I gravitate toward the kind of work that bridges technical depth with sharp positioning: helping teams understand not just what a product does, but why it matters and how to win with it.

Outside of work, I'm training for a half marathon, building a long-horizon vision around financial equity, and convinced that good systems are quiet acts of care.
